College of Liberal Arts and Sciences

BA POS

Bachelor of Arts in Political Science

The department of Political Science was established 1997, was a center of learning, with the principles of democracy, excellence towards progressive, responsible, state of the art, leadership in Caloocan City and the nation. The role of the department to develop students’ experts in politics and governance. Indicating the philosophy of responsible citizenship, in order to achieve progress and development with social justice and public service for all, through Academics excellence, scientific research and community service in Caloocan and in the Philippines. Presently there are 905 students in the department with 6 sections, producing 5 Supreme Student Council (SSC) President, 2 Crossroad editors-in-chief 5 cumlaude with graduates who became college professors, police officers, law students, HR personnel, high school teacher, call center agent, barangay officials, government officials and employees.

BA COMM

Bachelor of Arts in Communication

A four-year degree program that prepares and hones students’ competence to pursue career in the media industry particularly in advertising and public relations to meet the world’s changing needs. Students are being proficient in the skills necessary as a communication practitioner in various fields like broadcasting, print, theatre arts, cinema, and advertising. The program also tailors basic knowledge in the Business Process Outsourcing Industry (BPO) which includes integrated courses from TESDA in line with the CHED objectives to have an Outcomes-Based Education Program

BPA

Bachelor of Public Administration

This is a degree course requiring graduates to pass the Civil Service Examination to qualify for employment in government offices. The course is designated to prepare graduates to become effective and efficient public servants to man government offices all over the country. Only passers of the University Entrance Examination with an average of 83% better can quality to enroll. A grade retention of 2.25 is imposed to ensure quality graduates. Review lessons are incorporated to all subjects in the third graduation. Successful passers are assured of better career service in the government. BPA is offered in the both the North and South Campus.

BSCS

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Combines a core of Computer Science subjects with depth in supporting fields. Students gain strong programming skills and computing fundamentals that prepare them to adapt to new technologies and trends.

BSEMC

Bachelor of Science in Entertainment and Multimedia Computing

The study and use of computing concepts in the design and development of multimedia products — covering science, entertainment, education, simulations, and advertising. Students learn the full pipeline of Game Development and Digital Animation, building creative competency to lead original projects.

BSIS

Bachelor of Science in Information System

A 4-year hybrid program blending Computing Science with business essentials. Students are honed in designing, developing, and managing information system setups including transaction processing systems, automation systems, and enterprise-based systems.

BSIT

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

A 4-year hybrid course combining Computing Science fundamentals with business courses like management, accounting, and finance. Students are trained in designing, developing, and managing information systems such as transaction processing systems, automation systems, and enterprise-based systems.

BS MATH

Bachelor of Science in Mathematics

The undergraduate mathematics program of the University of Caloocan City comprised of general education courses and substantial mathematics courses applicable in Business, Engineering, Statistics and Technology. To comply with CHED CMO No. 19, s. 2007, the aforesaid program attempted for a change in nomenclature to BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN MATHEMATICS for its freshmen before the Technical Assessment of the Commission on Higher Education last September 28, 2012. However, because of the K-12 effect of the Basic Education Curriculum in the future, the attempt for a change of nomenclature and curriculum did not materialize. Thus, the Bachelor of Arts in Mathematics is still the current undergraduate mathematics program offered by the University while still waiting for a new CMO to be issue by CHED that is aligned with the K-12 program.

BS PSY

Bachelor of Science in Psychology

The Bachelor of Science in Psychology is a 4-year degree program that aims to equip the future psychologist with skills and knowledge demanded in the third millennium which embodies the total Academics and practical development of the individual to do their task after graduation fully well.
