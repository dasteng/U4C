ADMISSION

ONLINE ADMISSION AND ENROLLMENT PROCESS

APPLICATION PERIOD: MARCH 3 - APRIL 15, 2025

1. INITIAL ASSESSMENT

  • Visit and Log In: https://aims.ucc-caloocan.edu.ph/ucc/applicants/
  • Accomplished the required data/information
  • Upload the required documents

2. PAYMENT OF FEES

Log in using Applicant ID and Password sent to your email.

  • Wait for system response
  • Once approved, pay the entrance examination fee
  • Check the entrance examination schedule

3. EXAMINATION

  • Print and bring the examination permit on the day of exam
  • Proceed to the Examination Site

4. RELEASE OF EXAMINATION RESULTS

  • Check results at: www.ucc-caloocan.edu.ph
  • Attend F2F Pre-Orientation at the Registrar Office
  • Secure Interview Slip for department evaluation

5. ENROLLMENT

  • Submit original and photocopies to Registrar's Office
  • Fill out online enrollment form and pay Miscellaneous fee
  • Wait for official enrollment notice and print registration form

Requirements

  • Senior High School Card, Diploma
  • Good Moral Certificate
  • Proof of Caloocan Residency
  • PSA Birth Certificate
  • Medical Certificate
  • 2x2 I.D. Picture

Information to Provide

  • Full Name (Last, First, Middle)
  • Present/Current Address
  • Choice of Campus (North, South, Engineering)
  • Program Choice for Admission
  • Active Contact Number/s
  • Email Address

IMPORTANT RULES AND NOTICES

Note: Failure to correct errors within the given time frame may result in account deletion due to inactivity.

  • Ensure all information is accurate to avoid delays.
  • Regularly check your applicant portal for updates.
  • Strictly follow deadlines for each step.
